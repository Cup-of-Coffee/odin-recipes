Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is a hearty and delicious chicken noodle soup that grandma couldn't have made better. It really hits the spot on a chilly evening.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 stalks celery, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 3 1/2 cups egg noodles

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine water, carrots, onion, celery, parsley, salt, garlic salt, pepper, and thyme in a slow cooker and mix well. Set chicken on top and push down slightly into the liquid.
  2. Step 2: Cover and cook on High for 4 hours or Low for 8 hours.
  3. Step 3: Add noodles and cook on High until noodles are tender, 30 to 45 minutes.
